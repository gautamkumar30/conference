# CMS

# Classes

- **User**
    - Attributes: _id, Name, University/Organization, Email
    - Methods: RegisterForConference()
- **Conference**
    - Attributes: Title, Date, Venue, Description, PostedDate, Organizers, RegistrationAmount, Theme, Chairpersons, Speakers (optional), Papers
        - Methods: UpdateDetails(), CancelConference(), InviteChairpersons()
- **Attendee**
    - Attributes: UserId, ConferenceId, DateRegistered (metadata), PaperId
    - Methods: CancelRegistration(), UpdateDetails(), SubmitPaper()
- **Paper:**
    - Attributes: AuthorId, Title, PaperLink, TotalRating, Ratings (array with chairpersonId-rating pairs)
    - Methods: UpdateDetails()
- **Invitations:**
    - Attributes: SenderId, ReceiverId, Role, Timestamp (metadata), status
    - Methods: SendNotification(), ReceiveNotification(), AcceptRole(), RejectRole()
